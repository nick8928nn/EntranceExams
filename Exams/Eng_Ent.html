All Entrance exams after 12th for Engineering in India

Top 10 Entrance exams after 12th for Engineering in India

  • JEE MAIN

    • Joint Entrance Examination Main is the most popular entrance examination to gain admission to BE/BTech, BArch, and BPlanning programs. This competitive exam after 12th is conducted by the National Testing Agency to gain admission to IIITs, NITs, SPAs, and Govt. Funded Technical Institutes such as IIST. Qualifying the JEE Main also makes you eligible to sit for JEE Advanced.

  • JEE ADVANCED

    • JEE Advanced is the second leg of the JEE examination. It is conducted by the IITs for students aspiring to gain admission to undergraduate science and engineering programs at all the participating IITs, as well as other universities such as the IISERs, IIST, IISc, etc. that use it as the basis for admission. JEE Advanced scores are also used for admission to foreign universities such as the National University of Singapore.

  • BITSAT

    • Conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, The BITS Admission Test is amongst the most popular entrance exams after 12th Science. Its purpose is to screen students who aspire to gain admission to the Engineering, Pharmacy, and Information Systems programs at BITS Pilani, BITS Goa, and BITS Hyderabad.

  • VITEEE

    • Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Test (VITEEE) is an important competitive exam after 12th for students who are looking to gain admission to various undergraduate degrees offered by the 4 VITs across India.

  • MET

    • The Manipal Entrance Test, formerly known as the MU OET is an entrance exam conducted by Manipal university for admission into various Undergraduate engineering courses offered by the university. The exam is conducted each year in the months of July and AUgust in two phases in 80 different Indian cities and Dubai in online or computer based mode.

  • MHT-CET

    • MHT CET or Maharashtra Common Entrance Test, is a state level entrance test conducted by the Government of Maharashtra for candidates to take admission in the engineering, pharmacy and technology programs offered by private as well as government colleges in Maharashtra.

  • COMEDK

    • The Consortium of Medical Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) is one of the entrance tests conducted in India. The entrance exam is conducted to determine the merit of students seeking admission to the undergraduate courses in the Member Institutions of the Karnataka Professional Colleges Foundation Trust (KPCF). More than 150 engineering colleges offer more than 20,000 seats through COMEDK. Candidates who are passed or appearing for class 12 exams with the minimum required marks in mathematics, physics, and chemistry subjects are eligible to apply.

  • Karnataka CET

    • Karnataka CET (KCET) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the state of Karnataka to offer admission to various courses in Professional Educational Institutions in the State. Through Karnataka CET, candidates are offered admission to various streams, including Medical, Dental, Ayurveda, Homoeopathy, Engineering, Technology, Architecture, Naturopathy, Yoga, Agriculture, Forestry, Sericulture, Horticulture, Home Science, Agri Biotech, Veterinary Science, Animal Husbandry and many other courses. Candidates who are seeking admission to UG-level engineering courses through Karnataka CET must pass or appear in the class 12 exams with the minimum required marks in mathematics, physics, and chemistry subjects.

  • Kerala KEAM

    • Kerala Engineering Agriculture Medical Entrance Examinations (KEAM) is a state-level entrance test conducted by the government of Kerala for admission to colleges in the state for admission to Medical, Agriculture, Veterinary, Fisheries, Engineering and Architecture Courses. To be eligible for admission to UG-level engineering courses, candidates must have passed or are appearing in class 12 or its equivalent exams with the minimum required marks in mathematics, physics, and chemistry subjects. Candidates can fill up the application form on the official website by paying the required amount of application fee.

  • EAMCET

    • This is a state-level entrance test conducted by the state of Andhra Pradesh for admission to Medical and Engineering courses in various state colleges. On behalf of the state government of Andhra Pradesh, JNTU conducts EAMCET every year. Candidates who clear this exam are selected for admission to various engineering colleges located in the state. The eligible candidates can apply on the official website of JNTU.
