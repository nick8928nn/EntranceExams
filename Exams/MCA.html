Top 10 MCA Entrance Exams in India

  • NIMCET

    • NIMCET or National Institute of Technology Master of Computer Applications Common Entrance Test is an exam conducted for admissions to MCA programmes. This entrance exam is conducted every year to offer admission into Masters in Computer Application (MCA)Programme in participating NITs.

  • BHU PET MCA

    • Banaras Hindu University (BHU) conducts Postgraduate Entrance Test (PET) for admissions to its PG programmes offered by its affiliated/constituent colleges. The programmes are offered in Humanities, Fine Arts, Performing Arts, Science, Engineering, Commerce, Management, Mass Communication, Hospitality, Travel, and Teaching streams.

  • WB JECA

    • The West Bengal Joint Entrance Exam for Computer Application, commonly known as WB JECA is the exam for admission to MCA courses. The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) conducts the WBJECA every year for admission in the colleges of West Bengal. Candidates interested in MCA admission in West Bengal need to appear in WBJECA and qualify for the exam.

  • JNU MCA

    • The Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) offers admission to Master in Computer Applications (MCA) through JNU MCA entrance exam. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts the entrance examination under the name of Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Examination (JNUEE).

  • MAH MCA CET

    • Maharashtra MCA Common Entrance Test, commonly known as MAH MCA CET is the entrance exam for admission to MCA courses offered by various government and private colleges in Maharashtra. The exam is conducted every year by the Maharashtra Common Entrance Test Cell in online mode as a Computer Based Test. Candidates seeking admission in the MCA course in Maharashtra have to qualify the entrance exam.

  • PAMCAT

    • The Punjab Technical University Jalandhar conducts the Common Aptitude Test to short list candidates who have applied for the MCA & MBA courses of the university. The Punjab Admission to Master of Computer Applications Course is organized through the Common Aptitude Test every year in the last week of May for all the interested candidates. The entrance exam is open for all the eligible graduates in the concerned field by the university.

  • BIT Mesra

    • Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra conducts BIT MCA entrance exam for admission to 2 years of full-time MCA courses at institutes Mesra, Lalpur, Jaipur and Noida campus. The exam is conducted once a year in various cities across the country. The allotment of seats is done on the basis of candidates' performance in the exam through online counselling.

  • IPU CET MCA

    • IPU CET full form is Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test. Candidates striving for admission to various courses at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University will be admitted either through National Level Test conducted or through IPUCET conducted by the University or on the merit of the qualifying degrees depending on the course they want to apply. There shall be no CET for admissions to BTech/BA LLB/BBA LLB/LLM & BArch programme.

  • DU MCA

    • Delhi University is a Central University which most of the students across India aspire to be a part of. Admissions to various UG and PG courses along with that of Masters in Computer Applications are conducted by National Testing Agency in the name of Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET).

  • CG Pre MCA

    • The Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Chhattisgarh conducts entrance examination for Masters in Computer Applications, i.e. CG Pre MCA exam. Admission to almost 200 seats are offered through this entrance examination in the state of Chhattisgarh.
